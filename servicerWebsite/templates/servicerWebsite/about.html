What is the purpose of this App?

Servicer is a sustainability initiative designed to assist users with daily chores that they don't
like doing or don't have time to do. We are here to assist you in the exchange of services
with other users to make everyone's day better by having the tasks you may not like doing
taken care of by other users and in exchange you will provide a service.

By accessing our site you can search for services that are being offered by other users, and offer your services in return.
